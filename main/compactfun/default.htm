Compact Fun is a set of 31 games and puzzles. Brief descriptions of these are given below.

The categories shown on the Home page correspond to the names of apps that also contain the games/puzzles listed when you tap one of these. Compact Fun contains all the games and puzzles found in these other apps. Please note that an individual game or puzzle may be found in more than one of these categories/apps; for example, a logic-based puzzle will be found in Compact Logic and Compact Puzzles.

General help and information

Remember that each game/puzzle has its own Help feature, accessed through the ? button in the title bar. When in the Help, return to the main game/puzzle by pressing ? again or by pressing the Back button.

Use the device Back button to navigate back to the main menu or, further back, to exit.

For further details of TopAccolades products, press the area of text in the black field at the bottom just beneath the main game/puzzle.
